OSHA and NIOSH Info

The following is a collection of information from OSHA and NIOSH that is utilized by the Noise App:

Exposure Threshold: The highest decibel value that a person can be exposed to over an 8 hour period.
  1. OSHA Criterion Level: 90 dB
  2. NIOSH Criterion Level: 85 dB

Exchange Rate: When a personâ€™s decibel exposure increases by this amount the amount of time said person can be exposed to the new decibel level and still receive the same dosage over an 8 hour period is reduced by half.
  1. OSHA Exchange Rate: 5 dB
  2. NIOSH Exchange Rate: 3 dB
